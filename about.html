About

Obstacle course racing has been a big part of my life beginning in 2013 when I convinced a coworker to run the Spartan Beast with me. It was the worst experience of my life, and during the race I swore I would absolutely never do one again! But by the car ride home I was planning everything I could to make the next one better, I was officially hooked. In our day to day lives we often do not complete anything that is extremely taxing on our bodies and minds. I believe this leads us to an apathic lifestyle, one where nothing is ever really quite wrong or exactly right. We don't have the confidence to be successful in our endeavors because we have no idea of what we're actually capable of, and how far we can push ourselves to get there. Obstacle course racing provides this for us. We train our bodies in preparation to perform to the best of our capabilities, and get a chance to realize how strong we really can be. I am a former Spartan SGX coach, an ISSA certified fitness trainer, and have completed over 68 races in my career.
